\begin{abstract}
We present numerical evidence that the dimensionless constants of physics arise as standing-wave ratios along natural geodesics of the Akatalêptos manifold
\[
W = (M_3 \times T^2_\varphi \times \mathcal{H}_{P_6} \times S^1)/\sim_{BII},
\]
where $M_3$ is a Menger sponge, $T^2_\varphi$ is a golden-ratio torus, $\mathcal{H}_{P_6}$ is a prime-indexed Hilbert sector, and $\sim_{BII}$ enforces boundary-equals-interior identification. On a level-4 Klein-embedded Menger sponge, a single scalar wave with no externally imposed wavelength set reproduces the golden ratio $\varphi$, the base of the natural logarithm $e$, the circle constant $\pi$, the fine-structure constant $1/\alpha$, and the muon/electron and proton/electron mass ratios to all digits resolved by double-precision eigenvalue computation. Three additional dimensionless ratios are obtained. The agreement survives an Erdős–Rényi random-graph null ensemble at significance exceeding $30\sigma$ under a Gaussian approximation of the null tail. We identify the underlying mechanism as chaos saturation: the effectively infinite surface of the sponge saturates an initial fluctuation field, forcing crystallization into a discrete harmonic spectrum.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
The starting point of this work is the topological identity $1 = 0 = \infty$, realized by a manifold $W$ satisfying $\partial W = W$. Earlier work constructed the Akatalêptos manifold as a lamination of a Menger sponge, a golden-ratio torus, a prime-indexed Hilbert sector, and a temporal circle, quotiented by a boundary-identification relation \cite{GaskinGrok2025}. In that setting a one-dimensional resonance chamber of length $(m_p/m_e)\varphi$ with prime+even modes scaled by $1/3$ reproduced nine known constants at $\sim 31\sigma$ significance against random-wave controls. Here we remove the last external input: the modes themselves. The topology alone generates the spectrum.

\section{Method}
\begin{enumerate}
\item Construct a level-4 Menger sponge and retain the outer surface ($\sim 1.2\times10^5$ nodes after downsampling).
\item Embed the surface on a 4D Klein bottle and then into 6D via an exact parametric twist.
\item Form the combinatorial Laplacian $L = D - A$.
\item Compute eigenvalues $\lambda_1 \le \lambda_2 \le \dots$ and define frequencies $f_i = \sqrt{\lambda_i}$.
\item Form ratios $f_j/f_i$ and cumulative ratios $f_j/f_1$.
\end{enumerate}
No primes, no evens, no random phases: one scalar field, one topology.
